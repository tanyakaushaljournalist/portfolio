About

I'm a financial journalist with skills in data and multimedia. Currently, I work as a reporter covering European private markets with The Drawdown with a particular focus on tax, ESG, and AI.Before The Drawdown, I was a data reporter with Yahoo Finance in New York, where I covered US stocks, economy, politics, tech and infrastructure in a data-driven breaking news and long-form manner. I also have multimedia skills in audio-video broadcast learned through my undergraduate studies at City University of London and data skills obtained via my graduate studies at Columbia Journalism School.

Outside of work, I am an avid language learner. Trying to master Japanese these days.
